## 🔵 LinkedIn Post (Long-form Narrative)

Some creative partnerships are built on contracts and deadlines. 
Ours was built on a 20-year friendship.

Shane Vincent and I met when we were 13 years old at St. Peter's College in Sri Lanka. We didn't know it then, but those early years set the foundation for a musical collaboration that would span decades. 

I've watched Shane's musical identity take shape in real time. The early years of discovering voices, the middle years of obsessive instrument learning, and the later years where it all collapsed into a sound that is unmistakably his. 

As a producer, these aren't just memories. They are reference points. 

We just launched a new EP, but it isn't just another release. It’s a producer's tribute to an artist's journey. Each track draws from a distinct period of Shane's development, filtered through everything I've learned about music production, marketing, and tech in the years since. 

It’s a project that wouldn't exist without all three worlds colliding.

To anyone who has ever watched a friend grow into their creative potential and thought, *someone needs to capture this*—this is me doing exactly that. 

👇 Listen to the first two tracks (Near Love & Back on the 120) and read the full story below.
