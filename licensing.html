Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Commercial" use mean?

Commercial use means any project where money changes hands or the content promotes a brand, product, or service. If you are creating a video for a client, you need the Commercial tier.

Will I get copyright claims on YouTube?

Once you subscribe, you can whitelist your YouTube channel in your dashboard. Any claims on whitelisted channels will be automatically cleared within 24 hours.

How does the Tier 3 Base + Royalty option work?

For large productions with a PRO cue sheet requirement, you pay a smaller upfront synchronization fee, and backend performance royalties are collected via PROs (like ASCAP or BMI) when your film or show is broadcast.
