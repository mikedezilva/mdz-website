Mike De Zilva

Mike De Zilva is an entrepreneur, Web3 strategist, and digital infrastructure architect based in Colombo, Sri Lanka. With over seven years of experience operating at the frontier of the blockchain and artificial intelligence sectors, he specializes in building autonomous intelligence pipelines, architecting tokenized economies, and scaling decentralized infrastructure.

His work fundamentally addresses structural inefficiencies in global strategic intelligence, decentralized finance, and digital rights management—moving enterprise brands from speculation into high-conviction reality.

The Foundation

From Global Brands to Web3

Before engineering decentralized AI architecture, Mike established his strategic foundation managing international brands and large-scale consumer narratives. He served as a Brand Executive for ExxonMobil and later as a Brand Manager for Leo Burnett / Publicis Sri Lanka. This deep understanding of legacy market acquisition, combined with the realization that traditional reporting tools operate on fatal data latency, drove his pivot into advanced tech.

He holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) in International Business from Louisiana State University and a B.A. (Hons) in Business & Communication from Coventry University.

Creative IP & Music Tech

The Yelo Ecosystem:
Generative AI & Digital Rights

Leveraging his background as an established music producer and composer, Mike founded the Yelo Ecosystem to fundamentally reform how audio is generated, owned, and monetized in the digital age. By bridging the gap between high art and deep tech, he built a suite of platforms that solve the critical legal and quality bottlenecks plaguing the modern creator economy.

Yelo Studio

Generative AI Engine

Mike engineered Yelo Studio, an advanced digital audio workstation (DAW) and style-to-music generative engine built for content creators, freelancers, and enterprise media, designed to help music creators get from idea to product really fast. Unlike legacy AI platforms burdened by copyright infringement and metallic audio artifacts, Yelo Studio is architected to guarantee zero rights conflicts while natively exporting pristine, artifact-free 24-bit 48kHz WAV audio.

YeloPlay

Rights Tokenization

To secure the assets generated by creators, Mike developed YeloPlay. The platform tokenizes music IP for streaming royalties and enables DAO-based governance, backed by infrastructure grants from Elastos and BeatFarm.

Bitcoin Inscription

The Token2049 Inscription

In 2025, Mike served as the leading host for the 'Asia On-Chain' side-event during Token2049 in Singapore. In collaboration with Elastos, he orchestrated the world’s first live music performance explicitly inscribed onto the core Bitcoin blockchain—proving that high-fidelity cultural assets can be immutably secured without congesting primary network layers.
