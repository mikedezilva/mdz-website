Brand Story & Mission

The origin, philosophy, and strategic direction behind the Mike De Zilva brand.

Origin Story

From Leo Burnett/Publicis brand management and ExxonMobil brand architecture to founding Adpod (autonomous AI market intelligence) and the Yelo Ecosystem (generative music + digital rights). Based in Colombo, Sri Lanka.

Mike De Zilva combines an MBA from LSU and a BA from Coventry University with extensive experience in navigating both the creative and technical spheres of modern digital enterprise.

Mission Statement

"Building autonomous intelligence pipelines, architecting tokenized economies, and scaling decentralized infrastructure - while bridging deep-tech engineering with creative IP monetization."

Vision

Where the brand is headed: the convergence of AI, blockchain, and music. Pioneering on-chain music rights, autonomous data entities, and tokenized creative economies.

Brand Pillars

01
Innovation

Relentlessly pushing the boundary between what exists and what's possible. First-mover execution over incremental improvement.

02
Autonomy

Systems that self-correct, self-optimize, and operate without human bottlenecks. From neural pipelines to decentralized governance.

03
Precision

Every decision grounded in data. Every output measured against deterministic truth. Zero tolerance for noise.

04
Culture

Technology in service of human expression. Music, art, and identity as the ultimate application layer.

Target Audiences

Primary: Web3 / AI Ecosystem

Market makers, dApp developers, protocol teams, and decentralized finance professionals.

Secondary: Music & Creative

Artists, labels, rights holders, and content creators looking to monetize IP.

Tertiary: Enterprise & Media

FMCG brands, agencies, press, and conference organizers.
